Profile

Hi, my name is Ryan Hillard. I'm passionate about civic technology and making the world a better place. I've spent the last 5 years working for the U.S. Small Business Administration, working to improve their main property, SBA.gov, and migrating their systems to a cloud environment in Amazon Web Services.

I'm applying to Litnerd, because I feel like the company represents the perfect confluence of three things:

  • My professional experience managing technology programs for the last 7 years
  • My desire to work on something with a 'holy purpose' in the world
